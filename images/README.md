# Pump Images

Place your pump images in this folder with the following names:

- `lobe-pump.jpg` - Image of a lobe pump (blue, showing internal lobes)
- `screw-pump.jpg` - Image of a screw pump (metallic, showing internal screw mechanism)
- `centrifugal-pump.jpg` - Image of a centrifugal pump (teal-colored with electric motor)
- `gear-pump.jpg` - Image of a gear pump (blue, showing internal gears)

**Image Requirements:**
- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Recommended size: 400x300px or larger
- Aspect ratio: 4:3 or similar
- Background: White or transparent

The images will be automatically scaled to fit the cards while maintaining their aspect ratio.
